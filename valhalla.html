What is a black hole ?

A black hole is where gravity pulls so much that even light can not get out. The gravity is so strong because matter has been squeezed into a tiny space. There are three types of black holes. Stellar black holes are made when a star is dying. When the center of a very big star falls in upon itself, or collapses. When this happens, it causes a supernova. A supernova is an exploding star that blasts part of the star into space. Some of the smallest black holes formed when the universe began and supermassive black holes were made at the same time as the galaxy they are in.
